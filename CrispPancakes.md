### Ingredients: 
Sugar a bit less than 1/2 Cup  
1 Egg  
Vanilla Extract  
Yeast 1/2 Tsp  
Water 3-4 Tbs  
Flour 1/2 Cup  
Salt  

### Steps
Add Eggs, Sugar
Mix

Add Yeast, Water 
Mix

Add Flour, Salt
Mix

Fry on Medium Heat until Brown

### Filling:
Nutella, Milk, Roasted Pecans

### Topping
Caramel Butter Sauce
